Featured Project
While the company didn't have a clear existing brand identity, I utilized the colors in their old logo and gave them a fresh, brighter look. I chose and manipulated a display font that was bold with a throwback feel. While the brand features minimal imagery, I designed a wave pattern that is reminiscent of the swirls of mayo, mustard, or ketchup that you squeeze onto your sandwich. The logo can be reduced into a wordmark and a small logomark, and works with each of the brand colors.
